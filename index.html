About

About Me

I'm a design professional with 5 years of experience in graphics and front-end website content. I am an advanced Adobe Suite user, and I can navigate complex software. I got my start by doing set designs for live theater while obtaining a Bachelor of Arts in technical theater. After graduating, I ran lights and assisted with set up and strike for live concerts and comedy shows. I then obtained a Bachelor of Science in computer science and went on to work in Website Administration in Seattle. I am now seeking new opportunities!

Projects

Theater projects:

Set Design for Banker's Dozen 2009

Banker's Dozen was a student theater project performed in a small room without a budget. It was a comedy about banking. I utilized the university's prop storage reserve to select furniture, and I painted a giant, green dollar sign on the floor. It was memorable to see performers doing coordinated dance routines around the dollar sign.

Set Design for Waiting for Lefty 2010

Waiting for Lefty was another student theater project. It was a historically accurate play about a taxi union dispute so I added historically accurate details like wainscotting. Enlarged newspaper headlines sourced from library archives are on the wall.
